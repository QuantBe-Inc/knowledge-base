### Introduction
Pattern Functions is the essential suite, including Candle Patterns, Classic Patterns, Trending, and Crossover. This suite provides users with powerful tools to identify, analyze, and leverage market patterns. From recognizing specific candlestick formations to assessing prolonged trends, determining market direction, and pinpointing strategic entry and exit points, Pattern Functions equips you with the necessary features for a nuanced approach to shaping your investment strategies.

## Find Candle Pattern
Candle Patterns are formed by one or multiple candlesticks, offering a visual representation of market sentiment. These patterns serve as powerful tools for identifying potential trend reversals or continuations.
