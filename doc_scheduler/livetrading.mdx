###  Introduction
Live Trading enables real-time buying and selling of financial instruments. Users name sessions, choose a strategy, select stocks, and set the time frequency. The platform features an Auto Ending Scheduler for convenient session management. The page displays a graphical stock market performance summary and a Buy/Sell table with key details. Dropdown filters for strategy, ticker, and signals enhance data analysis. Experience precise execution and real-time decision-making with Live Trading.

## Simple Steps
Initiate a live trading by following these steps.
<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a New Strategy">
    Begin by crafting one or more strategies under "Create New Strategy" on the Dashboard page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initiate Live Trading Schedule">
    Head to the Dashboard and click on "Schedule Live Trading" to add a schedule.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Schedule Details">
    Complete the necessary details for your live trading session, including the session name, selected strategy, market, targeted stock, and preferred time frequency.
  </Step>
   <Step title="Activate Auto Ending Scheduler">
    Enable the Auto Ending Scheduler for convenient session management. You can do this by clicking the designated button or specifying the start and end times manually.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore Live Trading Performance">
    Once scheduled, explore the corresponding stock market chart and Buy/Sell table on the right side of the page. The chart provides a visual representation of your chosen strategy's impact on the market, while the table details key information such as time, ticker, signal, price, and strategy.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Auto Ending Scheduler
Users can activate Auto Ending Scheduler with a button click or set start and end times manually for precise session control.

<ResponseField name="Start" required>

</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="End" required>
